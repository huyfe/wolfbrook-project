About wolfbrook

Wolfbrook Capital Limited is a wholly owned subsidiary of Wolfbrook Property Group Limited.

Wolfbrook Capital Limited has been formed to raise funds from investors. Wolfbrook Capital Limited will lend those funds to other Wolfbrook Group members to help fund the business activities of the Wolfbrook Property Group.

What we do

Wolfbrook Property Group develops, manages, and holds a diverse portfolio of residential properties across New Zealand.

Our core focus is leveraging infill sites within city parameters that benefit from existing infrastructure. We research and purchase development sites in high growth corridors, considering local amenities like shops, schools and transport links.

We then build high quality architecturally designed turnkey homes for home owners and investors alike. Each development is a bespoke design to suit the location and with the purchaser in mind.
